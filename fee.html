Fee Calculation

We are happy to give free quotes and estimates for our services.


If you would like a fee estimate, please email us the type of service you are seeking (IRD, GST, Income tax, System/Website - setups) and submit to us. We will reply within 48 hours. Enter your details in the "Contact Us" section so we can provide a fee estimate either for any compliance requirements. e.g. GST, IRD, financial statements or any other strategic, business support, improvement needs.
