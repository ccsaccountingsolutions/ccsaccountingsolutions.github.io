Our Services

Compliance

Fulfil all legal requirements for all clients, small businesses and deal with Inland Revenue and other external parties to ensure all matters are resolved in accordance with regulations and in a timely manner. These services include preparing financial statements, tax, statutory returns, GST and FBT returns and business surveys.

Business Planning, Support and Improvement

We want to grow your business margins, reduce revenue leakages, identify savings/efficiences, develop growth opportunities with the right structural support/structure to achieve your financial independence/goals. Our focus is on the big picture as well as offering indepth analysis based on key drivers including best and worst case scenarios that will allow the right investment decisions to be made.

Property, Individual, Business Taxation

As your confidential business partners, we can assist with formations, annual returns, director resolutions relating to small to medium size business operations by offering regular managerial reporting (online if preferred), budgets, rolling forecasts or cash projections to enable business owners to focus on driving growth. As tax agents, we have expertise in dealing with Inland Revenue Department enquiries as well.

Manufacturing and Operational Value Chain Consultancy

Expertise in setting up costing systems and standards and reporting including setting up price models associated with formulating an effective operational solution can also be provided. Ensuring BOM's, COGS, PPV's and critical financial costings/returns are aligned will be a key enabler to improving performance, productivity and profitability.

Outsourced activities - Accounts Payables (PtP), Accounts Receivables (RtR) and Payroll

Depending on your business size, we can create an interactive platform for securely recording, approving AP invoices as well as preparing reconciliations. For AR activities, we can actively manage a collection programme of your ledger and effectively communicate its status on a timely basis.

Other - Legal, Re-Financing, Investment Property, media advertising, website creation services

We also have highly experienced and knowledgeable expertise to offer quality, reliable services in terms of setting up trusts, deeds, conveyancing arrangements, including dealing with council on consent approvals/advice. If there are other areas of expertise required, we are more than happy to link you to our extensive network of trained, experienced, qualified business consultants.
